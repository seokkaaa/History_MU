About Data

This data has been obtained from the Japanese colonial museum in Korea. By collaborating with the museum, we were successful in initiating a survey that would ask visitors of the museum about their overview and thoughts about the museum. The survey is categorized into four questions, where the survey would ask the visitors about their satisfaction scores, nationality, age range and personal opinions.

Furthermore in order to express their personal opinions through graphs, personal opinions were evaluated by utilizing the sentiment scores.

Statistics

Sentiment Score by Country

This data was obtained by asking a series of questions that determines someone's attitudes or sentiment toward the topic at hand. By using data science, people's responses were converted to a sentiment score. A score close to -1 indicates a negative sentiment, 1 indicates a positive one, and 0 indicates a neutral one.
